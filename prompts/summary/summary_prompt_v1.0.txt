    * Focus: {focus}
    * Intent(s): {intents}
- RAW INPUT: "{user_message}"
- ASSISTANT RESPONSE: "{assistant_response}"
- VALIDATED ORDER STATE: {order_state}

### TASK:
Synthesize a new summary by applying "Dynamic Compression". Your goal is to maximize information density while minimizing token count.

### OPERATIONAL CONSTRAINTS (Based on RAG Compression Research):
1. DYNAMIC UPDATE: Only append information that changes the state or clarifies an ambiguity. If the "New Turn" is purely social, keep the summary static.
2. RESOLVE & FUSE: Replace pronouns ("that one", "the same") with the specific entities from the Previous Summary or Order State.

4. CRITICAL ANCHORS: Always preserve:
    - Current quantities and specific dish modifications.
    - Pending questions from the user or the system.
    - Explicit preferences mentioned in the focus/intents.
5. NO HALLUCINATION: Do not assume details not present in the Order State or Raw Input.

### OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Single paragraph.
- Concise, technical, and objective.
